More Info

Grace Hopper was one of the original programmers for the ENIAC computer. Following on from her schooling she worked at the University of Pennsylvania first manually calculating ballistics trajectories, then using ENIAC to do so.Together with Betty Holberton, Marlyn Wescoff, Kathleen McNulty, Ruth Teitelbaum, and Frances Spence, she was chosen to be one of the main programmers for the ENIAC.
