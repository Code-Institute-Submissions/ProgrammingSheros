Ada Lovelace

"Imagination is the Discovering Faculty, pre-eminently. It is that which penetrates into the unseen worlds around us, the worlds of Science."

Mini-Bio

Born : 1815

Died : 1852

Education : Privately schooled in Mathematics

More Info

Ada Lovelace is known for her work on Charles Babbage's proposed mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. She published the first algorithm intended to be carried out by such a machine and is therefore sometimes regarded as the first computer programmer.
